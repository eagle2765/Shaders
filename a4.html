Description

1. Texture Generation (fragment shader)
I created a Mandelbrot Set. The colors chosen depended on what iteration of the formula the fragment escaped. Basically, the escaped colors contained the most colors in this order: blue > green > red.

2. Transparency (fragment shader)
I created random circle centers and their radii using a python interpreter, then hardcoding it into the fragment shader by putting them into one array. Then, I looped through every circle and if the pixel was inside the circle (using a formula), I would discard it.

3. Image Manipulation (fragment shader)
For Image Manipulation, I implemented brightness based blending. I took two pictures, and blended them based on the mouse's y position and an arbitrary window size. The blending formula includes the lightness computed from the rainbow colored image. Refresh the page if you have any issues.

4. Vertex Shader
I implemented warble by making the verticies move when the mouse's y position changed. I also used a sin function to make my polygons warble in the Y direction.
